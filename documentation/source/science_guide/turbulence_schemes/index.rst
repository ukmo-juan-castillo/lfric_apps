Turbulence Schemes available in LFRic
=====================================

Contained here are descriptions of the boundary-layer / turbulence schemes
available in the LFRic atmosphere model.  These schemes represent
sub-grid-scale mixing of momentum and scalars in the vertical,
and also in the horizontal for some schemes (usually only needed
at km-scale or higher model-resolution).
